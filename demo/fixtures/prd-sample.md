# Atlas Mobile: v1 PRD

## Summary

Atlas Mobile brings the Atlas project workspace to iOS and Android.
This document covers the v1 scope, success metrics, and rollout plan.

## Goals

Give existing Atlas users a fast way to capture tasks, review project
status, and respond to mentions from their phone. The mobile app is a
companion to the web app, not a replacement.

## Rollout

We will launch in all markets simultaneously to maximize the initial
press window. Marketing will coordinate announcements with the three
largest regional partners.

## Success Metrics

We will measure success by total signups in the first 90 days. The
target is 50,000 installs across both platforms, with app-store
ratings at 4.5 or higher.

## Core Features

### Capture

Users can create tasks with text, photos, and voice notes. Drafts are
saved locally and synced when a connection is available.

### Project Status

The dashboard shows each project's health, open tasks, and recent
activity. All data is synced in real time across devices, so the
mobile view always matches the web app.

### Notifications

Push notifications keep users engaged with mentions, due dates, and
status changes. Notification volume is tuned to avoid fatigue.

## Out of Scope for v1

- Offline editing of shared documents
- Time tracking and reporting
- Admin and billing settings

## Open Questions

Pricing for mobile-only seats is still under discussion with finance.
